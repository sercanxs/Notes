Web Sites 4 components:
1- HTML: The "bone structure" of your webpage
2- CSS: The part that makes your website look beautiful and full of colour etc.
3- JavaScript: The interactivity that happens when you click a button or you type in a box and something else happens etc.
4- Server-Side: This is the hidden code that executes on a server when your browser loads a website. Ex: Python, PHP, Node.js
